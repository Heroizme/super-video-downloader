<b>Features</b>
Free video downloader app lets you download videos from any website or social media platform like Facebook, Instagram, Twitch and more.
All work on the client
Supports M3U8 and MPD lists, mp4 streams and live streams
All video save as mp4 video
Cookies supported
HTTP proxy support
This app using network service for fetching adblock lists and list of supported sites(detection by url) for optimization
